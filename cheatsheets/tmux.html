Notes

  • ◆ = prefix (Ctrl Space)
  • Status: COPY PREFIX ZOOM indicators show current mode
  • Mouse on: drag to select, scroll to scrollback, drag borders to resize
  • Windows auto-rename to basename($PWD) · override with ◆ r
  • Detach-on-destroy off: closing last window switches sessions instead
  • History 50000 · OSC52 clipboard sync on
