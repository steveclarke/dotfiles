Notes

  • ◆ = Super (Win key). Shift/Ctrl/Alt escalate.
  • Numpad workspace keys mirror the top-row digits exactly — see the layout above.
  • Volume / brightness: media keys with OSD. Add Alt for 1% precision.
  • Resize keys: -/= for L/R, Shift -/Shift = for U/D (100px steps).
  • Forgot a binding? ◆ K opens the live keybindings menu.
